# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this application ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects information obtained from users who access the Service using Roblox OAuth authentication.

By using the Service, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Roblox OAuth, the Service may receive the following information from Roblox:

* **Roblox User ID**
* **Roblox Username**
* **Public Roblox Avatar Thumbnail**

This information is provided by Roblox through its official OAuth authentication system and is used solely to identify your account within the Service.

The Service **does not collect your Roblox password or login credentials**.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used for the following purposes:

* Authenticating users through Roblox OAuth
* Identifying users within the application
* Assigning and managing user roles or permissions
* Displaying your Roblox username and avatar within the interface
* Maintaining moderation or administrative records related to the Service

The Service only collects the **minimum information necessary for these functions**.

## 4. Data Storage

Some information, such as Roblox User IDs and usernames, may be stored in the Service's internal database to maintain user roles, permissions, or administrative logs.

We take reasonable measures to protect stored information from unauthorized access or misuse.

## 5. Data Sharing

We **do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties**.

Information may only be disclosed if required to comply with applicable laws or legal obligations.

## 6. Data Retention

User information is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the Service and maintain its functionality.

If the Service is discontinued or user data is no longer required, stored information may be deleted.

## 7. Third-Party Services

This Service relies on **Roblox OAuth** for authentication. When using the Service, you may also be subject to Roblox's own policies.

You can review Roblox’s policies here:

* https://www.roblox.com/info/privacy
* https://www.roblox.com/info/terms

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect information stored by the Service. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Users should take appropriate steps to protect their own accounts.

## 9. Children's Information

This Service does not knowingly collect additional personal information from children. All authentication is handled through Roblox accounts, which are governed by Roblox’s own policies regarding minors.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates occur, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document will be revised.

Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how data is handled by the Service, please contact the Service administrator.
